# Code of Conduct

Sparkz is a public, generative space. The bar is simple: be the kind of contributor
who makes the collective stronger.

## The standard

- **Generative, not extractive. Symbiotic, not parasitic.** Add more than you take.
- **Bring solutions, not just complaints.** Critique with a concrete path to fix.
- Assume good faith. Disagree on the work, not the person.
- No harassment, hate, or personal attacks. No spam, no shilling, no scams.

## Not welcome

- Harassment or discrimination of any kind.
- Speculation-as-the-product behavior: pump talk, moon promises, or pressure to
  tokenize. Sparkz leads with the work, not the coin.
- Bad-faith noise that wastes contributors' time.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove contributions and may block accounts that break
this standard. Report issues to the maintainer via the repo or at
[trysparkz.com](https://trysparkz.com).

Adapted in spirit from the Contributor Covenant, tuned to the Sparkz ethos.
