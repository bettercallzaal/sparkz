# Sparkz - live stream demo script

A ~5-7 minute walkthrough. Everything below is LIVE at trysparkz.com. Open the tabs
in this order before you go live.

## Tabs to pre-open
1. `trysparkz.com/demo` - the explainer (start here)
2. `trysparkz.com/c/cocconcertz` - a real brand Capsule
3. `trysparkz.com/c/testing-spark` - the sandbox (has boosts + a receipt)
4. `trysparkz.com/admin` - the Meme Engine (have the operator token ready)
5. `trysparkz.com/admin/empire` - the tokenless empire launcher

## The pitch (30 seconds)
"Every creator-coin tool makes you launch a coin on day one. That turns your
community into speculators before you've built anything. Sparkz flips it: you start
with a **spark**, not a token. Build backing and momentum first; a coin is optional,
later, if ever. Back the album, not buy a coin."

## The walkthrough

### 1. The idea - `/demo` (1 min)
Read the top: spark not a token. Scroll the loop (6 steps). Land the line: "the moat
isn't the token - it's the accumulating data." Set up that Sparkz is a live platform,
not a mockup.

### 2. A real Capsule - `/c/cocconcertz` (1 min)
"This is CoCConcertZ - a real ZAO brand, as a Capsule, not a coin." Point out:
- Identity + the **/zao** Farcaster chip (it has a real Farcaster home, fid 19640)
- The **Integrations panel** - Treasury (Empire), Token (Clanker), Email list,
  Bounties (POIDH), Farcaster, Agent - "a Spark is a hub"
- Stats + Meme Receipts - "this is the moat accumulating"

### 3. Back it live - `/c/testing-spark` (1 min)
On the sandbox: type an email in **Boost** -> submit -> the count ticks up. "Anyone
can back the work, free, no wallet. That's a real supporter in the data now." Hit
**Share** -> "Cast to Farcaster" -> show the composer pre-filled to the channel.

### 4. The Meme Engine - `/admin` (2 min - the money shot)
Unlock with the operator token. Pick a Capsule. In "Flag a cultural moment," type a
real moment (e.g. "CoCConcertZ just packed a surprise midnight show"). Submit ->
**3 real AI drafts appear** (Capsule-grounded, cheap tier). Pick the best -> Approve.
"That just wrote a Meme Receipt - the record of what moment, why it matched, and what
it earns. Do this every day and you've got a proprietary map of what creates value."

### 5. Graduate (optional) - `/admin/empire` (1 min)
"Only if the momentum's real: a Capsule graduates to a tokenless empire - a create2
treasury on Base, nothing on-chain until first interaction - or a token on the
Clanker rail. The coin is plumbing, never the pitch." Show the launcher UI (flame
preview, fields). Don't deploy live unless you want to spend the gas.

## Close (30 seconds)
"Sparkz is live today: 7 ZAO brands, Sign in with Farcaster, a Farcaster Mini App,
the Meme Engine, backing, and the integrations to graduate. The proof isn't 'we
launched a coin' - it's 'supporters created attributable distribution and we measured
it.' That's the spark."

Community Swarm (supporters remix + get attributed), the autonomous agent (ElizaOS -

Capsules for the ZAO brand audit.
